ABOUT US

We are a non-profit providing service to healthcare workers, safety guards/policemen, and the underprivileged across Gujarat.

We conduct cost-effective distribution drives in various cities of Gujarat, based on the donations we receive, not to forget that our volunteers add to the funds regularly. Our volunteers take all necessary precautions concerning the COVID-19 pandemic while they perform their duties.

HISTORY

The core idea behind WeHelpHelpers originated from a yearning to help the underprivileged section of our society who was the worst hit of the COVID-19 pandemic. Their deteriorating health and plight was the trigger that set our volunteers in motion.

Since then, our primary aim as a responsible citizen has been to work as a helping hand to the poverty-stricken section, to fight hunger and improve their mental health and well-being. Our volunteers are grateful to the healthcare workers, police force and other essential workers working on the frontline and have assisted them in whatever way possible.

Vision

Our vision has always been to uplift and bring up the lower class and help them through the tough times.

Our further plans are to help people get vaccinated and spread awareness, to educate and provide necessary education tools to underprivileged children, to provide hot meals to daily wage earners. Our final aim is to provide help and care to every unfortunate deserving soul we come across, alongside that we also aim to thank the helpers helping us by offering them the maximum of our efforts, so that they can continue with their work.

MISSION

We have always believed efforts bring positive change and make a difference in people's lives.

Deriving from the name itself our goal has always been to help the people who have selflessly helped us during the most crucial times of our lives. Our mission is to provide them the same comfort they have helped us to get. We also try our best to help by providing them the knowledge and commodity which is necessary for survival but not in their fortune.

SERVILE TO THE SERVERS

We at WeHelpHelpers believe in giving back to the community. We believe in kindness, compassion, and courage to lighten up the lives of others.

Through sheer hard work and dedication, we have shifted attitudes and raised our voices to provide commodities for the survival of the needy, and service to those who have helped us get through the pandemic. We are determined to capitalize fully on the donations we receive and have impacted a large community through the same.
